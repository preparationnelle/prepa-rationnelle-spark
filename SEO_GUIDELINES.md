# SEO Guidelines & Project Rules

## Quick Wins

- [ ] **Set your page titles and meta descriptions** to ensure search engines clearly understand each page.
- [ ] **Use clean headings** to give structure and meaning to your content.
- [ ] **Add alt text to images** so search engines can index your visuals.
- [ ] **Create readable URLs** so both users and engines instantly get the pageâ€™s purpose.
- [ ] **Enable your sitemap** so Google can easily crawl all your pages.
- [ ] **Keep images light and remove unnecessary scripts** to improve loading speed.
- [ ] **Connect a custom domain** so your site can build long-term authority.

## Long Term

- [ ] **Write clear, consistent content** so the model generates strong semantic signals.
- [ ] **Link your pages internally** to help search engines explore your whole site.
- [ ] **Publish fresh content regularly** to keep search engines coming back.
